Al Hilal Banking Demo deployed successfully!

Services running:
  - api-gateway      (NodePort :30080)
  - bff-retail-service
  - customer-service
  - account-service
  - kafka
  - redis

To get the gateway URL:
  minikube service api-gateway --url

To test login:
  curl -X POST http://\<gateway-url\>/api/auth/login \
    -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
    -d '{"email":"abdullah@test.com","password":"password123"}'

To check all pods:
  kubectl get pods
